vi cheat sheet
--------------

Edit:

- Insert at cursor                    i to edit, <Esc> when done
- Move to line end and Append mode    A

Delete:

- Delete one character                x
- Delete 4 characters                 4x
- Delete line                         dd

Quit:

- Quit vi                             :x
- Quit without saving changes         :q!
