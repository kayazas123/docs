Valgrind basics
---------------

Basic:

- Command line structure                  valgrind --tool=<toolname> <args> <program command>

Tools:

- Callgrind                               --tool=callgrind
- Memcheck (default)                      --tool=memcheck

Misc:

- Required for JVM profiling              --vex-iropt-register-updates=allregs-at-mem-access
